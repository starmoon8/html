3. Why Unhulled Barley Is Excellent (But Not Always #1)

Unhulled (hull-intact) barley keeps the bran and maximum beta-glucan. It’s one of the strongest natural whole foods for cholesterol, but you need to eat ~½–¾ cup cooked several times a week to hit effective doses. Powders above make it much easier to get the full 3 g beta-glucan daily.

4. Quick Tips for Maximum Results

  • Aim for 3 g beta-glucan or 7 g+ soluble fiber daily (easy with 3–4 servings of the powders).
  • Pair with a diet low in saturated fat and high in vegetables, nuts, and olive oil.
  • Track your numbers: re-test bloodwork after 4–8 weeks.
  • Cook unhulled barley like rice or in soups (pressure cooker = 30–45 min).
  • All options are safe for most people, but check with your doctor if you take medications.
